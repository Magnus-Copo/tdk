SKTEC Trust's Vision & Mission

SHREEMATI SHREE KAMALA & THIMMAPPA EDUCATIONAL AND CHARITABLE TRUST (R) is dedicated to creating leaders of tomorrow through traditional values and modern skills.

Our Vision

To provide opportunities to create Global Citizens and Leaders of Tomorrow through Traditional Values of Love, Empathy, Discipline, and Respect, and to Train the Skills required for Successful Interaction with Other Individuals.

Our Mission

  • Pursuit of excellence in Academics, Sports, and Human Values.
  • Instilling qualities of Self-Reliance, Courage, and Self-Esteem.
  • Nurturing physically, mentally, and spiritually balanced citizens.
  • Encouraging independent thinking and critical analysis.
  • Helping pupils discover clarity of thought and harmony with nature.
  • Inculcating the best of Indian Culture and tradition.
  • Creating responsible, disciplined, and secular citizens dedicated to service.
